    The Platform provides an ecosystem built on spaces that act as independent seller on the Platform.

    Spaces managed by space manager provide a showcase for exhibiting exceptional art created by handpicked, talented artists and to allow them to promote and showcase their artists.

    When subscribing to a space, a space contract is created. A space contract is a business relationship between a seller and the space. It defines the duration of the relationship and the commission rate applied to sales on the first or second market.

    Sales made through the Platform are contracted directly between the buyer and the seller. As such the Platform is not considered as a reseller of the artworks proposed by the seller.

    Monumental is however not responsible for the commercial success of each of the spaces managed by space managers.

    Under the Collaboration Agreement between the space manager and Monumental and regarding artworks to be promoted and sold on the first or second market, the space manager may use the Platform in order to:

    • Select artist/collector to become Member or guest of his space

    • Grant access to artist/collector to become Member or guest of his space

    • Identify artist /collector by ensuring the artist is who they claim to be

    • Inviting artist and collector to become space members or guests via direct invitation from their profile during the defined period

    • Ensure that artworks are consistent by considering the characteristics of those with the artist approach of the space and more generally the Platform

    • Certifying the ownership of artworks in digital format that will be published in his space

    • In relation to the first market (primary sale), certifying artworks are:

      • original, they were not the object of previous sales on other platform or marketplace, since newly created through the Platform
      • Certifying artworks are unique, artworks were or will not be associated to no other NFTs

    • Negotiate directly with artist fees and commission that will be applied during the artworkâ€™s sale on first and second market (secondary sales).

    • Ensuring all displayed artwork are legal and adhere to Monumental terms and conditions

    • Ensuring that any content made available by the space manager on the Platform

      • does not contain scenes of evident and/or gratuitous violence (physical or even moral)
      • is not false and/or unlawful or obscene, indecent, abusive, intimidating, defamatory, discriminatory
      • does not violate privacy nor intellectual property rights

    • The start and end period defined on that space
